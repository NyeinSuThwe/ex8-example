Autumn Beef Stew for Slow Cooker

Slow-cooker comfort with hearty root veggies — perfect for crisp evenings.

By Carlotta

Total cook time: 5 hr 15 min

Ingredients

  • Beef chunks (round steak), bite-sized
  • Flour (or gluten-free blend)
  • Beef broth
  • Carrots and potatoes, chopped
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Coat beef with seasoned flour in the slow cooker. ~6 min
  2. Add vegetables, broth, and seasonings; cover and cook (high 4–5 hr or low 10–12 hr). ~5 hr
